# Pimento Cheese

## Ingredients

- 228 g (8 ounces) Old Cheddar Cheese
- 114 g (4 ounces) Monterey Jack Cheese
- 114 g (4 ounces) Cream Cheese (room temperature)
- 1 4 ounce jar of pimentos, chopped
- 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
- 1/2 White Onion (grated)
- 1/4 t Garlic powder
- 1/4 t Onion powder
- 1/8 t Cayenne
- S & P to taste

## Directions

1. Grate 1/2 of each cheese on the coarse holes of a block grater, and half on the smaller holes.
2. Add everything else to a bowl and fold to combine.
